Non-commercial · Open source · Made by volunteers

Small tools, made with care, given away for free.

uncommercial.org is a hub for digital volunteers building projects for social good - open source, ad-free, and always with care to personal data.

What we do

We find small, real problems and build careful solutions for the people who have them - then share everything for free.

There's no business model here. Whatever we make is free to use, free to fork, and built by people who care about the cause more than the click - reducing waste, sharing trustworthy information, and lending a hand where it's needed.
